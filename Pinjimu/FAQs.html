    FAQs

    Who should host a Pin Party?
    Anyone looking to have a little fun with their friends can host a Pin Party.

    What should I expect at my Pin Party?
    You and your friends should expect to have a great time. Our manicurists will help you try out new techniques and styles that teach you how to do them easily on your own!

    Is there a cost of hosting a Pin Party?
    No, we will connect you to one of our trained and independent manicurists who will come to your home at no charge and help with the manicures. In fact, our hostesses enjoy a generous reward program paid for by PinPolish. We offer free nail spa sessions and discounts on manicure kits as a thank you for sharing!
